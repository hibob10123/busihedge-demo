Demo Mode Overview

Hedjee helps small fleet operators cap fuel spend volatility with simple weekly parametric protection contracts. In this demo mode, hover highlighted controls to see what each piece means.

How hedging works

  • Set a strike price (e.g. $3.15/gal) for your coverage.
  • Pay a predictable premium up front for a set volume.
  • If spot price > strike during coverage, you get automatic payout.
  • This turns fuel risk into predictable budget line item.

Main benefits

  • Protects margins when fuel spikes, so you can bid confidently.
  • Avoids surprise transport cost increases in your P&L.
  • Simple dashboard shows current exposures, settled contracts, and portfolio health.

What to do

  • Use Markets to pick a hedging product by fuel type.
  • Pick a duration & strike that fits your business risk tolerance.
  • Click Trade and confirm to lock in the protection.
